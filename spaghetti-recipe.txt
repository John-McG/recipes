Ingredients:
 - 1 pounds ground beef
 - 1 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 onion, chopped
 - 1 can tomato paste
 - 1 can tomato sauce
 - 2 cans tomato puree
 - 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon pepper
 - 1 box spaghetti noodles

Directions:
 1. Cook all ingredients except ground beef, onion, and noodles for 4 hours.
 2. Brown the ground beef and onion and drain off the excess fat.
 3. Add ground beef and onion mixture to sauce, and cook for 1 hour.
 4. Cook noodles in separate pot.
